About

VOXEL · A tiny browser‑born sandbox

This world runs on a lightweight voxel engine designed to mimic the feel of classic block builders directly inside the browser. Terrain, blocks, inventory, crafting, and more are handled entirely on the client.

  • Instant loading from a single HTML file.
  • WebGL‑powered 3D rendering with mouse‑look camera.
  • Breaking and placing blocks in real time.
  • Inventory, crafting, and furnace interfaces.
  • Support for creative and survival‑style play modes.
